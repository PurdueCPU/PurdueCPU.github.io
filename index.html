About Us


The Competitive Programmers Union exists to influence and create a Competitive Programming culture here at Purdue University. We aim to spur growth in the Purdue community’s involvement in the ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest (also known as the ICPC) and to help students develop skills needed to solve complex data structures and algorithms questions often presented in technical interviews.
